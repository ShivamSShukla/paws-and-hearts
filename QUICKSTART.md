## Step 1: Get Amazon Associate Tag (5 minutes)

1. Go to: https://affiliate-program.amazon.com
2. Click **Join Now for Free**
3. Fill in your information
4. When approved, go to your dashboard
5. Find your **Associate Tag** (looks like: `yourname-20`)
6. **COPY IT!** You'll need this!

## Step 2: Deploy to Vercel (3 minutes)

### Option A: Using Vercel Website (Easiest!)

1. Go to: https://vercel.com
2. Sign up with GitHub/GitLab/Email
3. Click **Add New** ‚Üí **Project**
4. Upload your project folder (drag and drop!)

## Step 3: Add Your Amazon Tag (2 minutes)

After deployment:

1. In Vercel Dashboard, click your project
2. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Environment Variables**
3. Click **Add New**
   - Name: `AMAZON_ASSOCIATE_ID`
   - Value: `yourname-20` (your actual tag!)
4. Click **Save**

**Your site auto-redeploys with your tag!**
